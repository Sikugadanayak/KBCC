About Us

Building Bright Futures in Denkana

KBCC – Knowledge Post Coaching Center is a dedicated tuition center located in Beribeli, Denkana, Odisha. We believe every student deserves personalized attention and a strong academic foundation.

Our center provides comprehensive coaching for students from Class VI to X, covering all major subjects with regular assessments and transparent result tracking for parents and students.
